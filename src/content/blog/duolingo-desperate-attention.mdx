Duolingo is the most famous language learning app on TikTok. There are several things they're good
at - today I'll cover the first one.

Just like any other app out there, Duolingo wants to be in front of you as much as possible.
The social media team behind the app realised that it's actually similar to being an
overly-attached partner. So they created a content theme around it. Here are 3 video examples:
