Evan spends most of the time explaining why some videos are getting 100 views.
His audience finds the advice very useful, hence why the post got 1.5 million views.

But the last couple of seconds are equally important. This is where he directly
asks people to follow his account if they like the content he's producing. An easy sell,
since he's already proven his worth.
