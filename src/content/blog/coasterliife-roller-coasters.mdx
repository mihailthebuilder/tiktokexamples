This video from Taj fetched **4 million views**. And he's done it multiple times
with his recordings of roller coaster rides.

The reason it works? There are plenty of people out there who like living
vicariously. Including young kids, who will especially be fond of theme park
experiences. And where will these kids will be looking for these experiences online?
You guessed it, on TikTok, where they form the largest demographic.
